ABOUT THE PRODUCT

Product Description

Click & Catch is a digital token-based claw machine booth designed for campus events. Students claim digital tokens from the website and redeem them at the booth to play the claw machine and catch capsule prizes.

Problem It Solves

Many campus booths fail to attract students because they only display products without interactive experiences. Click & Catch solves this by combining digital engagement with an exciting claw machine game.

Target Market

The primary target market is college students aged 17–22 who enjoy fun activities, collectible accessories, and personalized items during campus events and fairs.

Unique Selling Proposition

Click & Catch combines digital token registration, an interactive claw machine experience, and instant prize customization such as custom phone charms that students can create on-site.

Product Features

Campus Sticker Packs

Fun and stylish sticker packs inspired by campus life. Students can decorate their laptops, notebooks, water bottles, and gadgets with creative designs that represent their school spirit.

USB Port Plugs

Protect your device ports from dust and dirt using cute and functional USB port plugs. These small accessories help keep laptop and gadget ports clean while adding a bit of personality.

Crochet Cable Ties

Handmade crochet cable ties designed to organize charging cables, earphones, and USB cords. These eco-friendly and reusable ties help students keep their bags and desks neat and clutter-free.
